Built for the phone, not the web.

The customer experience is a phone call. No app, no login, no QR code. Your side is a single bookmarkable URL that shows call log + invoice, plus a daily text with the numbers. That's it.

01

Customer calls your number

Your business line forwards to a Twilio number after hours (or always). Caller hears a real-sounding receptionist in under a second.

02

AI handles whatever it can

Books appointments, quotes ballpark prices, exits gracefully on wrong numbers. Remembers returning callers by phone number.

03

Emergencies reach your cell

SMS brief lands before the call bridges. You pick up already knowing who + what + where. No "who is this?" handoffs.

04

You sleep. The agent learns.

10 PM daily summary. Every non-emergency call gets a YES/NO follow-up. Regressions flagged automatically overnight.
