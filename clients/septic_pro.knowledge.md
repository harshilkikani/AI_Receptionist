# Pricing

- Routine pump-out: **$475** for standard 1000-gallon concrete tanks.
- 1500-gallon tanks: **$525**.
- After-hours emergency surcharge: **$150** (before midnight) / **$250** (after midnight).
- Real-estate inspection: **$225 flat fee**, results within 48 hours.

# Drain field repair

- We replace drain fields on-site over **1–3 days** depending on size.
- Typical quote range: **$6,000–$12,000**.
- Free in-home assessment before committing to repair.

# New septic system installation

- Full new install: **$15,000–$22,000** for residential systems.
- Permits and soil percolation testing included in the quote.
- Timeline: 3–5 days once permits clear (usually 2–3 weeks).

# Service area

- Lancaster County and surrounding townships (Ephrata, Lititz, Willow Street, Strasburg, Quarryville).
- Limited availability in Berks and Chester counties — ask before assuming yes.

# Emergency response

- Business hours: 7 AM – 6 PM Mon–Fri.
- Emergency 24/7 for backups, overflows, sewage in basement.
- Typical on-site response time: **90 minutes** within service area.

# Maintenance recommendations

- Standard tank: pump every **3–5 years** for a family of 4.
- Heavy use or garbage disposal: every **2–3 years**.
- If it's been over 5 years, schedule one ASAP.
