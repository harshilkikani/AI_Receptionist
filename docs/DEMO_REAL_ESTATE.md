# Demo flow — real estate inbound

A ~3-minute live demo geared toward agents and small brokerages.
Different emotional arc from HVAC: the customer isn't in distress,
they're shopping. The win isn't speed, it's **lead capture + same-day
follow-up** vs. the call rolling to voicemail at 8pm.

**V11.0 — real estate is now a first-class vertical.** Open the demo
page, open the demo drawer (`⋯`), switch the industry selector to
**Lawrence Realty**. The chat caller list, suggestion chips, owner
phone label, and pre-baked owner SMS bubbles all swap to real-estate
content. No tenant config needed — the registry drives every surface.

## Pacing notes

- This demo is **softer** than the HVAC one — no emergency = no
  urgency = the win is captured leads, not speed.
- Show the unified history (Beat 3) prominently. Agents track buyers
  across multiple conversations, sometimes weeks apart.
- The "Worth a follow-up" section is the closer for this audience.
  Specifically point at the empty state's copy if it's empty: "if
  this WAS your line, the followups card would have last night's
  warm-but-stalled buyers waiting for a 60-second callback."

## What to avoid in the real-estate demo

- Don't make the AI sound aggressive on the listing question. Buyers
  hate hard-sell openers. The receptionist should sound like a
  helpful assistant who happens to know the listings, not a SDR.
- Don't show the Twilio number prominently — for real estate, the
  agent likely wants to keep their existing number and just forward
  after-hours. Mention the forwarding setup once, briefly.
- Don't promise more than the platform does. No "automated tours",
  no "we'll book the inspection too". Lead capture + scheduling is
  the scope.

## Variations to test on prospects

- **Property manager** (multi-listing): show "Worth a follow-up"
  surface as the daily triage view
- **Solo agent**: emphasize after-hours capture; the receptionist as
  "your assistant who never sleeps"
- **Team lead**: emphasize the SMS owner-brief — leads route to the
  right agent automatically based on the listing
